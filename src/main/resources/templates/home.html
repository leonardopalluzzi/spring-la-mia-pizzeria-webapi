Pizzeria Roma, since 1800

Since 1800, Pizzeria Roma has been bringing the authentic taste of traditional Italian pizza to our community. Passed down through generations, our passion for genuine ingredients and time-honored recipes makes every slice a true experience of Rome’s culinary heritage. We welcome you to enjoy a piece of history, served fresh from our oven to your table.
